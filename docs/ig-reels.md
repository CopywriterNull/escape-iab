# EscapeHatch — Reels scripts + shot list

IG reach is Reels-driven; statics build credibility, Reels drive discovery. Each below is
15–30s, shot on a phone or screen-recorded. Voice: plain, technical, confident — no hype.
Hook lands in the first 2 seconds or people scroll. On-screen text carries the message
(most watch muted). End every one with the same CTA card (post-09 / link in bio).

Handle: **@getescapehatch** · Link: **getescapehatch.com/l**

---

## Reel 1 — "Your IG ads don't open in Safari" (the proof) ⭐ start here
**Format:** screen recording of a real phone · **Length:** ~20s

- **Hook (0–2s):** on-screen text *"Your Instagram ads don't open in Safari. Watch."* over your thumb about to tap an ad.
- **0–8s:** Tap a link-in-bio / ad in Instagram. It opens in the in-app browser. Point the cursor/finger at the tell: no real address bar, the little `aA`/`•••` menu instead of Safari's UI. Text: *"This is Instagram's in-app browser — not your real one."*
- **8–15s:** Tap `•••` → *Open in Safari*. Same site loads in real Safari. Text: *"Real browser = real cookies, real checkout, and the session actually sticks."*
- **15–20s:** Cut to the CTA card. VO/caption: *"We do that automatically, for every visitor. One line of code."*
- **Caption:** "Do this on your own store's ad and watch where it opens. Then fix it → link in bio."

## Reel 2 — The persistence demo (the biggest benefit)
**Format:** split-screen screen recording · **Length:** ~25s

- **Hook (0–2s):** *"Why your retargeting can't find shoppers."*
- **Left = in-app browser, Right = real browser.** Add to cart on both. Text: *"Same shopper, two browsers."*
- **Mid:** Swipe away / close the app on both. Wait. Reopen. Left (in-app) = fresh, empty, logged out. Right (Safari) = cart still there, still logged in. Text: *"Days later, they come back…"*
- **End:** *"In the in-app browser, the memory is gone. Escape them out and it sticks."* → CTA card.
- **Caption:** "Persistent sessions are the whole game. Escape once, and they can find their way back."

## Reel 3 — "One line of code" (the fix, for founders/devs)
**Format:** screen recording of the install · **Length:** ~15s

- **Hook (0–2s):** *"This one script recovered 45% more revenue per visitor."*
- Show pasting the `<script src="getescapehatch.com/e.js">` snippet into the theme. Text: *"Paste one tag."*
- Cut to an ad click escaping in-app → real browser in a smooth beat. Text: *"Live in ~15 minutes. Zero performance cost."*
- End on the +45% stat card.
- **Caption:** "If you run paid social to a Shopify store, this is the highest-ROI 15 minutes you'll spend this quarter."

## Reel 4 — Talking head / bold statement (no screen needed)
**Format:** talking to camera OR big-text over b-roll · **Length:** ~20s

- **Hook (0–2s):** *"Stop sending paid traffic to the worst browser on the internet."*
- Beat 1: "Every click from your Instagram ads opens in an in-app browser. People don't shop there — it feels off, so they bounce."
- Beat 2: "Move them into Safari or Chrome and two things happen: they actually buy, and their session sticks so they come back."
- Beat 3: "That's it. That's the whole product." → CTA.
- **Caption:** "The in-app browser is a silent tax on every paid click. Here's the fix."

## Reel 5 — Results montage (social proof)
**Format:** fast cuts of the brand-result cards · **Length:** ~15s

- **Hook (0–2s):** *"+45% revenue per visitor. Measured, not modeled."*
- Fast-cut through the brand-result cards (Andar +38%, Cove +52%, G FUEL +47%…) with a punchy beat. Text: *"30 brands. Live A/B controls."*
- End on the logo wall + CTA card.
- **Caption:** "Every number measured against a live holdout. Your CFO can check the math."

### Production notes
- **Muted-first:** on-screen text must carry it; treat VO as a bonus.
- **Trim the intro:** no logo sting — open on the hook frame.
- **Consistent end card:** reuse `post-09-cta` / the CTA story so every reel ends the same.
- **Cover thumbnails:** set each reel's cover to a clean text frame so the profile grid stays on-brand.
- **Repurpose:** the same cuts post to TikTok and LinkedIn (B2B buyers live on LinkedIn).
